Drinking with diabetes is difficult.

Having fun should not be stressful or dangerous. When you are drunk, your liver cannot process external glucagon, meaning traditional life-saving measures are ineffective. Steady Sip is here to help prevent individuals from getting to the point of emergency treatment. Steady Sip provides hyper-personalized recommendations and educational resources to better prepare diabetics for their nights out!
